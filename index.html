CoVID-19 Info

1- CoVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by SARS-CoV-2 .

2- SARS-CoV-2 is a RNA virus .

3- CoVID-19 is named by WHO (World Health Organisation) , which's full form is "Corona Virus Disease - 2019" .

4- SARS-CoV-2 is named by WHO, which's full form is "Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome - Corona Virus - 2" .

5- SARS-CoV-2 was first detected at Wuhan city of China .

6- CoVID-19 was first appeared/reported on December of 2019 (According to WHO) .

7- The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are "Fever, Dry cough, Fatigue"

8- Symptoms of severe COVID-19 disease include: Shortness of breath, Loss of appetite, Confusion, Persistent pain or pressure in the chest, High temperature (above 38 Â°C).

9- Stay safe by taking some simple precautions : such as physical distancing, wearing a mask, keeping rooms well ventilated, avoiding crowds and close contact, regularly cleaning your hands, and coughing into a bent elbow or tissue. Check local advice where you live and work.

10- If you have any suspected symptoms of CoVID-19, immediately isolate yourself and inform to your local medical authority .



Face Mask Info

1- Wear face mask when you are at social place or at home where older peoples are present .

2- Wear face mask in proper manner .

3- Face mask must cover the mouth and nose properly .

4- Face mask can be 4 types of, these are : N99, N95, Surgical mask, Cloth mask .

5- If you are using cloth mask then it should be at least 3 layers of mask .

6- Do not use one face mask for long time .



Hand Hygiene Info

1- Wash hand in an interval with alcohol based sanitizer or with soap and water .

2- Wash your hand before touching eyes, mouth, nose and face .

3- If soap and water not available then use alcohol based hand sanitizer .

4- If your hands are visibly dirty then you must use soap and water to wash your hand .



Social Distancing Info

1- Make social distance of at least 1 meter .

2- Also use mask at social place and make distance of at least 1 meter .
